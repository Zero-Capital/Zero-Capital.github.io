authenticity ≃ success ≃ inspiration

Inspiration is unlocked when success is the norm.
 
Zero Capital (Fund I) plans to back 36 pre-seed companies with $10M and have over a third of them win. Zero Capital is funded by a small, diverse group who share these values, including successful founders, nerds and OG investors.
 
Zero refers to "zero-to-one" and the aspiration to add value beyond capital. We operate with authenticity, transparency and positive reputation. We seek inspiration and joy as a deeper goal (and it's a nice signal for superlative execution). The ≃ symbol refers to interconnection of these values.
 
Zero Capital is founded by Adam Sah (25 patents, early@Google, 3 IPOs, 6 winners as angel investor) and the fund pays respect to Adam's father, mathematician Chih-Han Sah, who studied interconnections and transformations.
